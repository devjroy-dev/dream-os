## 1. WHAT THIS IS
Vendors run Instagram ads and read their IG analytics from inside the TDW vendor app.
"Connect Instagram" (official Facebook OAuth) → TDW surfaces their post/reel insights
(views, engagement, follower trends) in the Studio and offers one-tap promotion of
portfolio posts ("Boost to engaged couples in <city>, ₹<x>/day") — the vendor's own ad
account, the vendor pays Meta directly; TDW is the steering wheel, never the wallet.

## 2. WHY IT EARNS A BLOCK (the business case, recorded at parking)
A seventh revenue stream hiding inside the fifth: "featured promos" graduates from
promotion-inside-TDW to promotion-across-Instagram, run from the same Studio the vendor
lives in. A Prestige/Signature tier-anchor ("run your IG ads where you run your
weddings"). Compounds the n8n acquisition motion (we already build demos from their IG;
connecting the real account is the natural next handshake).
